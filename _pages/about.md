I am an incoming Master's student in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Arizona State University (ASU), where I will explore my interests in controls, computational fluid dynamics(CFD), robotics and autonomous systems. 

I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur (IITK). During my time there, I developed a strong interest in applied mathematics, computing, and machine learning. I am particularly fascinated by how these fields can be applied to mechanical systems.

Currently, I am interning at Coulomb AI, where I focus on simulation of Electric Vehicles and quantifying aging characteristics of Li-ion batteries. Previously, I was an intern at IISc Bangalore where I worked on the design, modeling and simulation of a vapor-separator which was adopted by ISRO for its potential implementation. 

I am also currently working with Prof. Leixin Ma at ASU on building a multitask and multifidelity gaussian grocess model(MMGP) for flapping NACA0012 airfoil performance optimization. I develop Julia code with the Waterlily package to build 2D and 3D simulations to minimize high-fidelity data costs.
